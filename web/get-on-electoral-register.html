Part of Vote in UK elections

Contact your local Electoral Registration Office

Contact your local Electoral Registration Office to opt in or out of the ‘open register’ - the version of the electoral register that’s available to anyone who wants to buy a copy.

They can also tell you:

  • where to send your register to vote form
  • where to send your postal vote or proxy vote application form
Postcode lookup

For example SW1A 2AA

Find a postcode on Royal Mail's postcode finder

See the About my vote website if you live in Scotland, or the Electoral Office for Northern Ireland if you’re in Northern Ireland.

Last updated: 15 May 2018
